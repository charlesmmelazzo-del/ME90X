Coach Notes

Evidence Rules Used

  • Train major muscle groups at least 2 days per week, with daily movement when recovery is good.
  • Use 2-3 work sets for most movements, with 6-12 reps for muscle and 8-12 reps for general strength.
  • Increase machine load after two good exposures with clean form.
  • Most sets should stop before ugly reps. You should usually feel like you had 1-2 good reps left.
  • Every fourth week backs off a little so your joints can catch up.
